Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbl Spoons Cacao
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 Slab Crushed Chocolates
  • 6 Cups Cake Flour
  • 3 Eggs

Instructions

  1. Mix all dry ingredients together
  2. Mix all wet ingredients into dry mixture
  3. Mix with blender until dough
  4. Bake in oven on 200 degrees for 45 minutes
  5. Cool of on drying grid and enjoy with a nice coffee!
d

Notes

These cookies are best enjoyed with a cup of coffee or tea. They can be stored in an airtight container for up to a week.

Feel free to add nuts or dried fruits for extra flavor!
